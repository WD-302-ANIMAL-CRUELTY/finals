10 CRUELTY-FREE FILIPINO BEAUTY BRANDS

The Humane Society of the United States released a docu-style short film titled Save Ralph in April. The video quickly went viral, drawing attention to the horrors of animal testing through the character Ralph, a bunny who has suffered multiple injuries as a result of his "job." With over 11 million views on YouTube, the film sparked a worldwide animal rights movement, increasing demand for cruelty-free products. Fortunately, these aren't difficult to come by in your area. Long before the now-popular short film was even shared, a slew of Filipino beauty brands pledged to be cruelty-free. If you've been considering donating to this cause, keep reading for a list of ten cruelty-free beauty brands and their must-have products.

1. Happy Skin

Happy Skin began selling their products in some branches of the popular retail store Plains & Prints when it was founded in 2013. They now have stores in all of the country's major department stores, as well as several boutiques of their own. Happy Skin believes that make-up products can help you feel more beautiful, and it is dedicated to providing skin-loving cosmetics to Filipinos.


Happy Skin Fresh Morning Dew Collection

2. Sunnies Face

Sunnies Face has quickly gained a large following since its launch in August 2018, which is quite impressive considering they only had lip products in their portfolio at the time. The brand didn't branch out into other categories like highlighters and brow products until months after their launch. The Fluffmate is the brand's newest product, which was just released recently.
Sunnies Face Fluffmate

3. Colourette

Colourette broke into the local cosmetics industry with a line of high-quality, low-cost lippies that have become one of many Filipinas' "holy grails." The brand has attracted so many fans thanks to its gorgeous shades and long-lasting lipsticks that it has expanded its product line to include highlighters and blushers.
Colourette's Shimmertint

4. BLK Cosmetics

Anne Curtis, actress and style icon, has launched her own make-up line to share her love of beauty. With BLK Cosmetics, a brand that focuses on "uncomplicated beauty," the celebrated actress hopes to redefine the local make-up scene. The goal of the brand is to keep things simple by going back to basics, with a carefully curated product selection that only includes high-quality make-up essentials.

blk cosmetics Tutorial: K-Beauty Gradient Lips by Anne Curtis, youtube.com

5. Human Nature

Human Nature was founded with the goal of restoring productivity to impoverished communities by creating high-quality cosmetics with natural ingredients sourced locally in the Philippines. Human Nature has grown into a major cosmetics company, but the company remains committed to its original mission of assisting communities and improving the lives of Filipinos.
Human Nature's Tinted Lip Balm

6. Ellana Cosmetics

Ellana Cosmetics, which was founded in 2008, is the Philippines' leading mineral cosmetics brand. Ellana Cosmetics was created specifically for Filipinas and offers sophisticated yet affordable mineral make-up that is specifically formulated for Southeast Asian skin. When developing their products, they took into account factors such as our tropical climate and skin tones.


Original Glow BB Foundie With SPF50 Mineral SkinShield

7. Pink Sugar

Pink Sugar is a homegrown make-up brand that was founded in 2014 with the goal of providing high-quality cosmetics at a reasonable price. Make-up, according to the brand, is about feeling more beautiful and confident as well as looking good.


Pink Sugar's Eye Shadow Palette, Eyebrow Kit, Lip & Cheek Tint, Setting Powders

8. Chihuahua Cosmetics

Changing the make-up game is Chihuahua Cosmetics, the first ever mini make-up in Asia. There’s a lot to love about this brand’s products. Aside from being adorably bite-sized, their products allow us to put a lot more make-up products in our bags so we can completely retouch our looks anytime, anywhere.


Chihuahua Cosmetics, New Arrivals

9. Paprika Cosmetics

Beauty, according to Paprika Cosmetics, should be unpredictable. As a result, the brand has created an eyeshadow palette with bold and experimental shades to help you create a look that is completely unique to you.


Paprika Cosmetics Eyeshadow Palette

10. Strokes Beauty Lab

Strokes Beauty Lab was already well-known for their brow microblading and lash services before launching the cruelty-free makeup line in 2020. Given that history, it's no surprise that this company is known for its high-quality and innovative brow products, particularly the Microblade Pen Perfector.
Microblade Pen Perfector
